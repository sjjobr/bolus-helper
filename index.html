🎤 How to talk to it

Anywhere you see a box, you can talk instead of type: tap the box, then tap the little microphone 🎤 on your keyboard and say it.

Say a whole meal. In the “Say or type your meal” box, say what you’re eating and tap Add it:

“Big Mac, medium fries and a Coke”
“Ten piece nuggets and a Gatorade”
“Two slices of pizza and chocolate milk”

Say your blood sugar too. Add it right in the sentence — “my blood sugar is 180 and a banana” — or tap the BG box and say the number.

Know the carbs already? Just say them: “forty five carbs” or “25 grams.”

Always check the list. It shows everything it added with a × to remove. Then tap Calculate — and remember, Loop has the final say.

How the math works

Carb dose = carbs ÷ carb ratio.
Correction = (BG − target) ÷ sensitivity.
Suggestion = carb dose + correction − insulin on board, rounded to 0.05 u, never below 0, never above your max bolus, and never when BG is under the safety limit. This mirrors how Loop thinks, but Loop is always the final word.
